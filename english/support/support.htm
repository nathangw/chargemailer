Issue / Question

Possible Solution

 

Where is the Setup Button?

The setup button is on the opposite of the board where the white triangle is pointing. It is right next to the USB-C connector labeled wall. When it is pressed, there should be a click sensation and the red light will come on.

 

What wall adapters are compatible?

ChargeMailer should work will all USB power adapters that can deliver 15W or higher. All USB-C and all USB-A adapter that are rated for fast charge or USB-PD.

 

Why are there so many ChargeMailer Reset emails?

The wall adapter is probably too small and is cutting off the power during charge.

 

Why does the phone or computer give a check charge connection warning?

ChargeMailer limits charging to a slow charge to encourage longer charging and less screen time. When the phone trys to negotiate a fast charge, it gives an error when ChargeMailer won’t allow the higher charge voltages. The connection warning can be ignored. If the connection were truly bad, the phone/computer will not charge.

 

The red LED light won’t come on when connecting power.

Make sure the side labeled wall is connected to a working usb wall adapter.

Where is the firmware file in the setup Update button?

ChargeMailer does automatic internet updates. The update button in setup doesn’t need to be used.

Can ChargeMailer send texts instead of emails?

Texts cost around 5 cents per message. Sending texts would require a monthly service plan instead of free emails. Contact support if you want have ChargeMailer send texts on your own account.

What is the warranty?

Product warranty is 1 year from the date of purchase for normal usage. Water damage and dog mauling is not covered.

Will ChargeMailer connect 5Ghz networks?

No. The hardware limits the connection to 2.4Ghz WiFi networks only.

I don’t see my network name on the WiFi list.

Try refreshing the page by swiping down. Try moving closer to the router to complete setup. Try typing in the network name manually exactly how it appears on other devices.
