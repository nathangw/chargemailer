ChargeMailer Instructions

 

Step 1) Plug the ChargerMailer side labeled &quot;Wall&quot; into a USB type C wall adapter.

 

Step 2) Press the Setup button on ChargeMailer. The LED light will turn on. This will turn on the wifi portal.

 

Step 3) Go to the wifi connections on your phone and click on the ChargeMailer_Setup wifi signal. Setup is the same for Android and iPhone.

 

Step 4) Once the phone connects, click on it again to enter the portal for ChargeMailer setup. If you see a popup to sign in, click on it.

 

 

Step 5) Click Configure WiFi.

 

Step 6) Choose your own WiFi signal and enter your own WiFi Password.

 

Step 7) Choose a different language if needed. The language will change after wifi is connected and an automatic internet update.

7a) Enter a unique name for the Charger such as your childs name. Do not use apostrophe &#39; because of html reasons.

7b) If a wireless charging station will be used, check &quot;Use Wireless Charger&quot;. This is important for detecting when the phone is attached.

7c) Enter the receiving email address where the charging and removed emails to go to. A second email can be added.

7d) Special Command is for debugging purposes.

7e) Click Save & Exit. The red light should turn off. If all is correct, the red light will stay off and not blink any error codes.

 

 

 

 

Error Codes:

If there is a problem sending emails or connecting to wifi, ChargeMailer will blink an error code on the LED. Setup can be done at any time on the phone by pressing the setup button and following the above steps again.

Read the Error Code by counting the blinks after the pause. The blinks cycle over and over.

 

1 Blink - WiFi is Not Connected. Possible Causes: Check that your WiFi is working. Check the password by re-entering it in setup. The signal range should be full range like any other wifi device.

 

4 Blinks - No phone has been charged in 24 hours. This really isn&#39;t an error and is just an indicator that no one is using ChargeMailer at night.

 

5 Blinks - The recipient email address wasn&#39;t accepted by the email server. Do setup again and check that the email address is a working email without any punctuation other than the @ symbol.

 

6 Blinks - Email Credentials are not found on the GitHub server. Contact the factory.
